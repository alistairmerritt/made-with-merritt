Pivot Hardware

Meet the Pivot Dial.

A tactile enclosure for the Home Assistant Voice Preview Edition. Designed around touch, feedback and everyday placement.

Tactile control

The VPE enclosure,
reimagined.

Pivot Dial reshapes the Home Assistant Voice Preview Edition around the way it’s used within the Pivot ecosystem – with the dial brought forward for tactile control, the LED ring more clearly defined for glanceable feedback, and the overall form designed to sit naturally in the home.

Rather than feeling like a device to tuck away, it becomes a more deliberate control object: something you can reach for, read at a glance, and leave out in the open.

Placement and orientation

A better position
for everyday control.

Pivot Dial is designed around two body styles and a set of mount options, so the VPE can sit where it makes the most sense for your space.

Choose the upright body for a more visible, angled control point with cleaner cable concealment, or the shortened body for lower-profile desk or wall placement. Depending on your room and setup, changing the orientation may also help the microphones hear you more effectively.

The firmware lets you set the LED orientation to match the body and mount, so the light ring displays the right way up in any position.

Customisable

Printed your way.

Pivot Dial is made to be 3D printed, giving you more control over how the hardware looks, sits and feels in your space.

Keep it quiet and minimal, give the dial a stronger presence, or choose a mount and body style that better suits the room. Small choices in colour, material and placement can change the character of the device without changing how it works.

Because the files can be modified, Pivot Dial can keep evolving, whether you want to refine a detail, adjust the form, or adapt it to your own setup.

Transparent by design

Know what you're installing.

Pivot is built for people who care how their smart home works. It's local-first, ESPHome-based, openly documented, and designed to keep the existing Voice Preview Edition functionality intact.

The firmware builds on the official Home Assistant Voice Preview Edition ESPHome configuration, while the integration and dashboard remain separate layers you can inspect, modify, remove or roll back if needed.
